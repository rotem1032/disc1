Minigames Commands for every minigame can be found in the Commands page.

Akinator

Can you beat the all knowing Akinator at his all time favorite game, guessing?

Win Reward: 350 Exp, Gamer Badge.
Average Time: 1 minute

Hangman

In this classic minigame, you and your friends will try your best to save a man from being hanged by guessing a specific word.

Win Reward: 250 Exp, Gamer Badge.
Average Time: 1 minute

Draw and Guess

After starting this game, you will get a DM from PEEKY with a white_canvas PNG and a word that you have to draw under a specific amount of time.
Once you're done drawing, just post your drawing to the initial channel for others to guess.
The person to guess the word the fastest wins the round.

Win Reward: 250 Exp, Gamer Badge.
Average Time: 1 minute
Guess the Song

In this minigame, you and your friends have to guess the name of a random song selected by PEEKY!
The first person to guess the song's name wins the round.

Win Reward: 100 Exp, Gamer Badge.
Average Time: 30 seconds

Trivia Questions

You and your friends can play this minigame by answering some trivia questions from random categories.
The person to answer a question the fastest wins the round.

Win Reward: 50 Exp, Gamer Badge.
Average Time: 15 seconds
